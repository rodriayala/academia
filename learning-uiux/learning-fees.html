Online mode for fees payment

As a continuous attempt to reduces the inconveniency of paying fees by cheque, the management has decided to collect the fees through online portal, 'Connecting Millions'.

You can pay the fees choosing any one of the following payment modes:

  • Net Banking - Parents can pay fees by login in to their bank accounts and make payments.
  • Credit/Debit card - Parents can make the payment by entering Credit/Debit card details.
  • UPI - Parents can enter their virtual payment ID or active connected phone number i.e. VPA to pay the fees.
  • NEFT/ RTGS - Parents can pay the fees by clicking the NEFT/ RTGS.
  • Easy Pay - we are using 'PayNow' payment gateway by American-XR Bank

On successful completion of transactions, a receipt gets generated, and parents can download it for their record.


*The all Fees here does not include cost of uniform, books and transportation. The School does not provide these products/ services and parents are free to get them from open market.
